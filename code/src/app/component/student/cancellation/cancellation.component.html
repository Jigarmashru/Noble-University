Cancellation And Fees Refund Policy

Cancellation of Admission:
  • For the cancellation of admission, a candidate has to apply in the prescribed form duly signed by him/her and counter signed by his/her parent/guardian at respective Institute.
  • The candidate has to enclose various required documents along-with form to be submitted to the Institute.
Refund of Fees:
  • The refund of fees as applicable shall be made as per rules & regulations.
