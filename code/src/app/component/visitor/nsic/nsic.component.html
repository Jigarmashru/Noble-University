About Noble Start-up & Incubation Center(NSIC)

Under the visionary leadership of the Board Members and with the grant of Rs. 1 Cr. under SSIP 2.0 by Government of Gujarat, Noble University has established Noble Startup and Incubation Centre (NSIC), which will act as a catalyst to promote startups by creating an ecosystem of innovation and incubation to transform creative ideas into business models for solving societal issues.
Noble Start-up & Incubation Center(NSIC),it is a A startup incubation center, or simply an incubator, is a supportive environment designed to help early-stage companies grow and succeed. These centers provide a range of resources and services to help startups overcome common challenges and accelerate their development.
the goal of a startup incubation center is to create an environment that fosters innovation, accelerates business growth, and increases the chances of startup success. Entrepreneurs benefit from the resources, guidance, and networking opportunities offered by these centers as they navigate the challenges of building and scaling their businesses.

Vision

  • To be a catalyst for student-driven innovations, transforming creative ideas into thriving startups that address real-world challenges and drive economic and societal progress.

Mission

  • Cultivate a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship among students.
  • Establish a student-centric innovation and incubation ecosystem.
  • Nurture students for startup success and societal impact.
  • Strengthen university capacity and mentoring processes.
  • Promote Intellectual Property awareness and support filings.
  • Implement interventions based on best practices for Mind-to- Market journeys.

Long-Term Goals

  • Sensitize around 5000 students from NU and other colleges/schools for innovation and entrepreneurship
  • Create an incubation center within Noble University campus
  • Support to student-led PoCs/Prototypes
  • Assistance to IP filings
  • Build capacity and support system for students and other people interested in innovation
  • Utilize existing resources at NU to create environment for innovation
