About Training and Placement Cell

Noble University has a separate Training and Placement Cell managed by a dedicated Training and placement team. TPC is a platform for the Training and Placement of students and acts as a window between students and outside organizations. TPC establishes contacts with industrial organizations to arrange training and placement of the students. The TPC guides and helps pre-final and final-year students in securing a rewarding career with their knowledge and achievements, by organizing campus Interviews and exploring various avenues for their placement. TPC had MoUs with many industries to train the students for placement. The University extends all possible help and provides all available infrastructure facilities to the organizations and their respective officials conducting campus interviews on the college campus.
