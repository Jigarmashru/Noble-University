We aim to move to the next level by sharply focusing on our ability to innovate. Through partnerships, we are transforming the way education is perceived and taught as a discipline while bringing a diverse population of students to our programs. Our programs teach in an engaging way to create, advance, and build the future. Our students tell us that Noble University offers an educational environment that is unique and what makes us distinct is the value and quality of our programs.

ASSOCIATE CO-MANAGING TRUSTEE

Mr. Siddharth K. Pandya

It is a pleasure to portray my view to you.

In my view, and as reflected in our couple of decades journey in educational & non-educational sectors good education is that which enables the growth and development of the child in multiple dimensions. While we realize the great need for improvement both in our school and higher education field the public education system as it is called, is to teach children from all parts of the country without any discrimination, and so to clearly serve the most disadvantaged sections of our society. so we decided to establish the not-for-profit Noble University, for educating professionals, meaning experts in education and other human development areas with the hope that many of our graduates would be committed to working in the social sector.

Good education is not about rote memorization and getting good grades, it is about giving our students, the ability to think critically, to question, and to develop their individual autonomy.
